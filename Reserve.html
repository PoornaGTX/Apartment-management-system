GENERAL CANCELLATION POLICY:

The reservation can be cancelled totally or partially but the following penalties must be paid: 100% if cancellation is made after one month of reservation period.
80% if cancellation is made in between 3 weeks after reservation period.
30% if cancellation is made in between a week after reservation period.
0% if cancellation is made in between 3 days after reservation period.

TERMS OF PAYMENT:

The reservation cancellation fee can be done by credit/debit cards.
The remaining fee is due at the check-in.
