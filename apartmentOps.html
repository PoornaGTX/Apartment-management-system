Real estate facilities require much thoughtfulness. These apartments are resident's homes.For this reason NIVAHANA APARTMENTS ensure regular and effective operations management for all residents.

Maintaning common areas which are exposed to residents and guests is an important
aspect.


These maintenance tasks include;

  • Always ensuring outdoor gardens and parking area is clean.
  • Keeping halways clear for the comfort and easy access of the residents.
  • Assigning a security detail to the entrance.

Contact details of licensed professionals for electrical,gas,water or plumbing services
will be provided
for the safety of the residents.


For your safety, we encourage you to,

  • Get help from licensed workers for apartment fixtures.
  • Always supervise your children, do not leave children unattended.
  • Do not lean out of windows or balconies.
  • In case of an emergency please proceed to the nearest exit as instructed.

Residents are responsible for the cleaning of their own apartments Garbage disposal units are located
within every two floors. Reidents will be given access to the security feed for their own apartment.
Guests should be accompanied by residents when using common facilities and free areas.
